Falls - More Common Than You Think

In Canada, falls account for approximately 63% of injuries reported by seniors and are the leading cause of injury-related hospitalizations [1, 2]. Delayed response to falls has been shown to greatly hinder the results of medical assistance [3]. This issue is important to us as Saanich has one of the highest over 75 age demographic on Vancouver Island and the number of residents over the age of 75 is expected to increase 79% by 2033 [4].

Micro-Doppler

FallSafe uses advanced technology to detect micro-doppler shifts associated with falls. Using machine learning algorithms it can determine if a fall has occured. The proof-of-concept is based on the ADALM-PLUTO SDR system from Analog Devices [5].

Machine Learning

Artificial intelligence determines if a fall has occurred. A softmax regression classifier is paired with stacked autoencoders to create a deep learning network for optimized detection, based on recent work by Jokanovic et al. [6]. This approach was also compared to support vector machine (SVM) classifiers with manual spectrogram feature extraction, as seen in other works [7,8]. The SVM achieved 90.9% accuracy while deep learning approach achieved 93.9% accuracy.

Emergency Contacts

Emergency contacts can be contacted in the event of a fall. In any case, emergency services will be contacted if FallSafe is certain that there was a serious fall.

Privacy

FallSafe cannot capture images, only the speed of movements, making it safe to use in private areas - unlike a camera. Falls can occur anywhere in the home, including the bedroom and bathroom.

Why Choose FallSafe?

Take a look at all these reasons!

Privacy Protection

FallSafe ensures full privacy for the user seeing as it does not require the use of cameras. Users can feel comfortable installing FallSafe in more privacy sensitive areas in their home or facility.

Accuracy

With the use of machine learning algorithms, Fallsafe has achieved a fall detection accuracy of 93.9%.

Peace of Mind

Users can be confident that they will be informed when a loved one has fallen. If a fall takes place, FallSafe conveniently sends messages to the emergency contact list for the user

No other devices required.

FallSafe is a unique fall detection system as it is one of the only non-wearable devices on the market. Users don’t have to deal with the burden of constantly wearing a device in order be safe in the event of a fall.
